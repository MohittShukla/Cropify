Cropify: Empowering Indian Agriculture

Cropify is our humble attempt to empower farmers across India with the tools they need to thrive. By predicting crop yields with accuracy, we aim to help farmers make informed decisions, optimize their resources, and increase their productivity. Together, we believe we can create a brighter future for Indian agriculture.

Cropify leverages a Decision Tree Regressor model with an impressive 95% accuracy rate to predict crop yields. By analyzing key factors such as state, district, year, crop type, and area, this model provides farmers with reliable predictions. With its ability to handle various types of data and capture complex relationships, the Decision Tree Regressor in Cropify is a valuable tool for farmers seeking to optimize their crop planning and increase productivity.
